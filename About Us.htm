About Us

The completion of Mavida bed and breakfast lodge ushers in a new era of quality accommodation in Bronkhorstspruit. Mavida provides temporary lodging by creating a differentiated experience capitalising on personal service. 

The lodge is built in a contemporary style and guests can enjoy the comforts of home away from home. All of our guest rooms have unsuited bathrooms. A spacious lounge and dinning area ensures our patrons enjoy a delicious scrumptious breakfast and dinner. The lodge offers hospitality warmth, tranquility and delicious food for both leisure and business travelers.
